WHAT IS WERO?

Wero offers everything you love about iDEAL, but is built for Europe. It comes with all the security and easy payments of iDEAL, with new options and services like purchase protection. Plus Wero is available in other European countries, so you can pay overseas as easily as you do at home.


Why the change?
Wero comes with the vision to unify payments across Europe, adding new services that match how people pay today and tomorrow. Designed for Europe, Wero is a future-proof independent payment solution. Soon, you’ll be able to use Wero in-store as well as online.

PHASE 1: INTRODUCING WERO

The first change you’ll spot is the new iDEAL | Wero logo. But relax, nothing else changes yet. During 2026 we’ll be busy working behind the scenes to set up the new Wero system. Meanwhile, just keep using iDEAL as normal.

But some things never change. Paying with Wero will be as safe, seamless and secure as you’re used to with iDEAL.
